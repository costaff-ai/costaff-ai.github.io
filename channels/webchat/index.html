CoStaff WebChat OSS

A browser chat
for your AI staff.

A lightweight browser interface — no Telegram, Discord, or LINE account required. Drop it on your own subdomain and let your team chat with the agents straight from the browser.

01  Highlights

Just a browser, nothing else.

Everything you need to hand work to your AI staff from the web.

No third-party account

Just a browser. Email + password login with identity hashing built in — nothing to install, nobody else in the loop.

Persistent conversations

Your conversation stays put across reopen, so you can pick a thread back up exactly where you left it.

Async results, pushed back

Long-running tasks finish in the background and push their results — files included — straight into the chat.

Self-hosted

Runs on your own subdomain, on your own machine. Open source, so you can read and modify every line.
