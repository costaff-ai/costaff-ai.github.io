CoStaff Platforms

One account. Eleven business platforms.
All agent-operable.

A self-hosted business suite built to be run by your AI staff. Every platform ships its own MCP server — so CoStaff agents can post a voucher in ERP, look up a customer in CRM, or open a helpdesk ticket on your behalf. The full suite is private source — contact us for access.

Suite-wide governance

Single Sign-On

A self-hosted, Keycloak-grade identity provider — OIDC, PKCE, MFA, Passkey. One login for the whole suite.

Per-app roles

The same person can be a sales rep in ERP and read-only in CRM — all assigned from one central place.

Three-portal interface

Employee, manager, and admin portals — each platform renders the right view automatically based on permissions.

Editable permission matrix

Role × module read/write rights, adjusted right in the UI and effective immediately — no redeploys.

Notification center + personal API tokens

Instant alerts on approvals and assignments. Personal tokens carry the holder's own permissions — nothing more.

Agent-operable by design

Every platform ships an MCP server plus a paired agent, so your AI staff operate it the same way you do.
