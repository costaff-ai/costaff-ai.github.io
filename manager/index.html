CoStaff Manager

The one you talk to.
It runs your whole staff.

CoStaff Manager is the orchestrator at the heart of CoStaff — the single assistant you chat with. It reads your request, routes the work to the right specialist agents, schedules reminders and recurring tasks, and delivers the finished result back to your conversation.

01  What it does

One conversation, a whole team behind it.

You talk to one assistant. The Manager quietly puts your staff to work.

Chat with one assistant

Reach it from Telegram, Discord, LINE, or the built-in web chat — one place to hand off anything.

Routes work to specialists

It reads what you need and hands the job to the right agent — coding, business analysis, or any agent you add.

Hub-and-spoke orchestration

Manager → agent A → Manager → agent B → Manager → you. Specialists collaborate; you only see the clean result.

Reminders & recurring tasks

"Remind me at 3 PM" or "summarise AI news every morning" — it schedules the job and pushes results to your channel.

02  Built on open standards

The open core of CoStaff.

No lock-in — the Manager is open source and speaks the same protocols the whole ecosystem does.

Google ADK

Built as a Google ADK LlmAgent — a pure orchestrator pattern, ~30 lines of wiring.

A2A protocol

Talks to specialist agents over the open A2A protocol, so you can plug in your own.

MCP tools

Uses MCP servers for scheduling, notifications, file handling, and more.

Self-hosted & open

Runs on your own machine as the CoStaff core. Open source under AGPL v3.

03  Get started

Run your own Manager.

Three commands from zero to a working Manager and staff — entirely on your own hardware.
